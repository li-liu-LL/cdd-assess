# Final Self-Check

The phase completion gates in `SKILL.md` are the canonical quality criteria; they were checked as each phase completed. This pass catches regressions and checks what only exists at the end. If any item fails, revise the bundle/report or mark the unresolved issue for human verification.

## Gate Re-Verification

Re-verify every phase completion gate against the *finished* workspace — later phases can invalidate earlier gates (a late-added figure breaks the arithmetic check's exhibit count; a reworded paragraph drops a citation). Pay particular attention to gates 4–6.

## Delivery-Only Checks

- Analytical body sentences are about the customer, documents, people, values, or facts — not about the report itself; each part's closing compliance assessment follows the observation-card discipline.
- Part I satisfies `references/risk-and-recommendation.md` end to end: framework disclosure, cited factor inputs, EDD trigger table, register-backed conditions, exactly one recommendation, rationale covering its six elements, draft banner, and compliance-channel note.
- The report nowhere states that the customer is approved, rejected, or onboarded; `scripts/check-decision-language.py` passes on the final HTML.
- No report source line cites scripts, pipeline files, or review-bundle paths; no bundle or report content was generated by a case-data script.
- The report is marked internal compliance use only, and this exact disclaimer appears on cover and final page:

> Drafted by an AI assistant. The risk rating and recommendation are draft proposals requiring review and approval by a qualified compliance officer before any onboarding action. Not a regulatory determination.

- Render-check artifacts (PDF and screenshot from `file://`) were inspected per the gate-7 list, and the exhibit register count equals the number of figures rendered.
- `review-bundle/status.md` is set to `delivered` as the last workspace action before the final response.

## Final Response Contract

When delivery is complete, respond with:

1. Report path and version.
2. Review bundle path.
3. Client pack name and customer type.
4. Render-check result.
5. Counts of material conflicts, gaps, register items, and human-verification items.
6. Draft overall risk rating, EDD determination, and recommendation — stated as drafts pending compliance officer approval.
7. Any unresolved report-critical limitations.
8. Reminder that the rating and recommendation are drafts and the compliance officer's documented sign-off is the decision.
