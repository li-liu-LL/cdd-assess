## Notes

- Use `CON#` for conflicts, `G#` for gaps, and `I#`/`D#`/`C#` for register rows.
- Do not use source IDs as conflict, gap, or register IDs.
- Status examples: `needs human verification`, `evidence limitation`, `baseline gap`, `grounding gap`.
- Every register row traces back to a conflict, gap, or checklist absence and carries that row's search trail.
- Conditions precedent in the report reference register refs; a condition without a register row is a composition defect.
